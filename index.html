Stand-up Comedy Open mics in the Twin Cities.

To add a new mic or make changes to this page, fork this repo and submit a pull request.

This github help page tells you everything you need to know about creating forks and submitting a pull request.

There is a spreadsheet of non open mic gigs. please contact them directly for add/remove requests. contact info should be in the sheet. https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1CjE_SjglmgoZ6He0ZyvU63ETccQeNODvfDQcEbLXLkw/edit#gid=0

    • Booker: Mike Brody
    • Notes: Pre-booked showcase, typically with a 2-6 week waiting list depending on how many people have signed up ahead of time. Comics get five minutes of time. Also, each week there are three "Wildcard spots" available for people who didn't sign up ahead of time. If you get to the HoC before 7:15 I will put your name on a list and draw three names. Those three people will get the 1st. 2nd and 3rd spot, respectively, after the MC.

    • Booker:Ira Ford
    • Notes: Doors open at 7:30. A showcase for comics by comics with a mini contest called the Amature Six.
